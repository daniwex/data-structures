Stacks implements LIFO (Last In First Out)

Has three methods:
1. pop() - removes the last item
2. push() -  adds an item to the end of the item list
3. peak() - view the topmost item

Queues use FIFO (First In, First Out)

has three methods
1. enqueue() - similar to above
2. dequeue() - similar to above
3. peek() - similar to stack
